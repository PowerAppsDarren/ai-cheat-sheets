Video Prompting Cheat Sheet

Storyboard & Video Generation

1. Start with the Big Picture 🎯

  • Objective: Define your goal (e.g., "Explain app flow", "Hero’s journey", "Sell product").
  • Audience & Mood: Specify who’s watching and desired emotional tone (e.g., "Teens, playful", "Execs, inspiring").

2. Define Your “Scene Recipe” 🥘

  • Action: What happens in the shot?
  • Characters/Objects: Who or what is featured?
  • Setting & Mood: Environment, lighting, and vibe.
  • Camera Movement: Pan, zoom, cut, POV.
  • Duration: Approximate length of the shot.

3. Add Directorial Flair 🎥

  • Cinematic Style: e.g., "Pixar animation", "2D comic".
  • Tone Presets: e.g., "Slapstick humor", "Orchestral drama".
  • Transitions & Effects: e.g., "Glitch effect", "Hand-drawn overlay".

4. Keep It Structured & Modular 🧩

  • Use numbered lists or bullet points to separate scenes.
  • Label scenes clearly (e.g., "Scene 1: Hook", "Scene 2: Problem").

5. Show & Tell with References 🌟

  • Reference Styles: e.g., "Like Apple product ads", "Wes Anderson symmetry".
  • Include sample text or dialogue for Voice-Over/on-screen copy.

6. Prompt Anatomy Template 🧬

  • Title / Purpose: Brief video title and goal.
  • Audience: Who and tone.
  • Overall Style: Visual and audio style.
  • Scene Breakdown: Numbered scenes with timing.
  • Directorial Notes: Special effects or transitions.
  • Voice-Over / On-Screen Text: Exact copy.

7. Common Pitfalls & How to Dodge Them 🐉

  • Too vague: ❌ "Make a cool video." → ✅ "Storyboard a 10s sci-fi UI tap sequence."
  • Overloading prompts: Break complex sequences into separate prompts.
  • Missing details: Always specify mood, style, and pacing.

8. Advanced Hacks ⚡

  • Progressive Refinement: Start broad, then detail each scene.
  • Conditional Prompts: Define style consistency rules (colors, effects).
  • Parameter Tweaks: Experiment with creativity/temperature settings.

9. Sample “All-In” Prompt 🎁

  • “Storyboard a 20s tutorial for a mindfulness app aimed at professionals:
  • - Style: Pastel 2D illustrations, calm lo-fi music
  • - Audience: 25-40 year-olds seeking stress relief
  • - Scenes: Messy desk → App open → Breathing animation → Relaxed user
  • - Notes: Cross-dissolve transitions, floating particle effects.”
